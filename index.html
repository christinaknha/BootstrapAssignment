About Us

Here at Chrissy's Kittengarten, we strive to provide a warm, loving, and relaxing environment for the felines at CMPD's Animal Care and Control. We foster felines of various ages, ranging from a few days old to 12 years old. It has become a collaborative effort and we couldn't do it without the support of people like you. If you would like to learn more about the fosters we have available or how you can help, join our community by clicking the button!
